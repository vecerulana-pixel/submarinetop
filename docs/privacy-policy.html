Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Submarine Tap (“the App”), published by vecerulana-pixel.

Submarine Tap is a casual arcade game. We respect your privacy and aim to keep data collection to a minimum.

Information We Collect

The App does not require users to create an account and does not ask users to provide personal information such as name, email address, phone number, address, or payment information.

The App may process limited technical information necessary for app functionality, security, diagnostics, and delivery through Google Play. This may include device type, operating system version, app version, crash information, and basic usage or performance data provided by platform services.

How We Use Information

Any information processed by the App or platform services may be used to:

  • provide and maintain the App;
  • improve app stability and performance;
  • detect and fix technical issues;
  • comply with Google Play requirements and applicable law.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Limited technical data may be processed by service providers or platform services that help distribute, maintain, analyze, or secure the App, including Google Play services. These providers process data according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Permissions

The App may request device permissions only when needed for app functionality. If a permission is requested, you can allow or deny it through your device settings.

Data Retention

We do not maintain user accounts or store personal profile data on our own servers. Technical data handled by third-party platform services may be retained according to their own retention policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information associated with the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed specifically to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

User Choices

You may stop all data processing by uninstalling the App. You may also manage app permissions through your Android device settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through the GitHub repository issue page: https://github.com/vecerulana-pixel/submarinetop/issues.
